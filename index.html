Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

🔒 ConvertX processes everything locally — no files ever leave your device.

1. What We Collect

ConvertX does not collect, store, or transmit any files you process. All operations happen entirely within your browser. No file data is sent to any server.

We do not collect:

  • Uploaded files or images
  • Personal information or account data
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Usage history or logs

2. Cookies & Local Storage

ConvertX uses browser cache solely to store AI model weights (~40 MB) so they don't re-download each visit. No tracking cookies are used.

3. Third-Party Services

  • jsDelivr / cdnjs — JavaScript libraries (no tracking)
  • Google Fonts — typography (Google may log requests per their Privacy Policy)

If Google AdSense is enabled in future, this policy will be updated.

4. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.
